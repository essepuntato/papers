Abstract

This documents introduce the accompanying material for the paper submitted to ADS 2017 entitled Towards accessible graphs in HTML-based scholarly articles by Silvia Mirri, Silvio Peroni, Vincenzo Rubano, Paola Salomoni, and Fabio Vitali.

Materials
